Risk framework

How we make capital decisions

Our process is designed to reduce narrative bias, enforce downside discipline, and allocate only when expected returns justify risk.

1) Isolate governing variables

We reduce complex systems to the variables that drive pricing, supply response, and regime behavior.

2) Validate across regimes

Assumptions are tested across historical periods, policy settings, and geographic markets before capital is committed.

3) Define risk before execution

Each deployment includes explicit invalidation levels, downside scenarios, and monitoring triggers.

4) Review and adjust

Post-allocation reviews are used to improve repeatability, position sizing discipline, and portfolio resilience.
