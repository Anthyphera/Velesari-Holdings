Strategy

Capital deployment framework

The strategy process combines market structure analysis with explicit risk governance before any capital is committed.

1) Market structure analysis

Each opportunity begins with structure mapping: supply-demand drivers, inventory behavior, basis dynamics, and regime sensitivity.

2) Physical system constraints

Storage, transport capacity, processing throughput, and logistics bottlenecks are assessed to determine whether pricing is structurally supported.

3) Risk limits and invalidation

Risk limits, invalidation levels, and scenario boundaries are defined before execution to maintain downside discipline.

4) Counterparty and operational review

Counterparty standards, legal terms, operational readiness, and documentation quality are reviewed before entering commitments.

5) Portfolio supervision

Positions and partnerships are reviewed against original assumptions, with sizing and exposure adjusted when evidence changes.
