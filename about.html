Firm mandate and ownership posture

Velesari Holdings is a privately funded, privately held commodity trading house allocating proprietary capital where physical constraints, delivery pathways, and contractual terms can be underwritten directly.

What the firm is

A private trading house and capital allocator focused on commodity-linked exposure with internal decision authority.

Capital is proprietary. The firm does not operate a public fund platform, brokerage portal, or external fundraising program.

What the firm is not

The website is not an advisory service, brokerage portal, or solicitation channel for securities, funds, or managed accounts.

No market commentary on this site should be treated as transaction advice.

Leadership and approvals

Tai Velesari, Managing Director, oversees allocation boundaries, governance standards, and commitment approvals.

Institutional relationship model

The firm works with commercial counterparties, suppliers, and operating partners able to support documented terms, transparent diligence materials, and clear execution responsibility.
