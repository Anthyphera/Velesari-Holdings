About Velesari Holdings

Velesari Holdings is a private investment holding company focused on commodity-linked opportunities where structural drivers are measurable and risk can be explicitly managed.

Mandate

We pursue opportunities across commodity markets and supporting infrastructure where disciplined execution and downside-aware underwriting can compound value over time.

Operating posture

Capital is allocated with defined decision rights, scenario testing, and continuous portfolio review. We prioritize durable relationships with counterparties that demonstrate operational reliability and alignment.

Leadership

Tai Velesari serves as Managing Director and is responsible for firm strategy, risk governance, and long-term capital stewardship.
