About

Firm overview

Velesari Holdings is a privately funded, privately held trading house and capital allocation firm focused on commodity-linked markets.

Private capital and commodity systems visual

Mandate

The firm allocates proprietary capital across commodity-linked opportunities where market structure is observable, downside can be defined, and execution discipline can be maintained over time.

Primary market focus

Energy and Agriculture are the core focus areas. These markets are assessed through supply-demand balance, transport and storage constraints, inventory behavior, and policy sensitivity.

Secondary capability set

Velesari maintains selective exposure to adjacent sectors including Metals, Logistics, Infrastructure, and commodity-linked operating partnerships where underwriting standards and governance requirements are satisfied.

Capital allocation approach

Capital is deployed through a combination of market execution, strategic ownership, and operating partnerships. Position sizing, invalidation levels, and review cadence are established before deployment.

Leadership

Tai Velesari serves as Managing Director and oversees firm strategy, risk governance, and long-horizon capital stewardship.
