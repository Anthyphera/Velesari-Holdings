About

Firm mandate and ownership posture

Velesari Holdings is a privately funded, privately held commodity trading house allocating proprietary capital in markets where physical constraints and contractual terms can be underwritten directly.

Private capital and commodity systems visual

What the firm is

An independent merchant house and capital allocator focused on commodity-linked exposure with internal decision authority.

Capital is proprietary. The firm is not a public fund platform and does not operate an external fundraising program.

What the firm is not

The website is not an advisory service, brokerage portal, or solicitation channel for securities, funds, or managed accounts.

No market commentary on this site should be treated as transaction advice.

Leadership and approvals

Tai Velesari, Managing Director, oversees allocation boundaries, governance standards, and commitment approvals.

Institutional relationship model

The firm works with commercial counterparties, suppliers, and operating partners able to support documented terms, transparent diligence, and clear execution responsibility.
