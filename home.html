Market Focus

Energy and Agriculture are primary operating sectors

These sectors are core because pricing is directly shaped by physical system constraints, seasonal cadence, and route-level execution capacity.

  • Energy systems: generation fuels, transport exposure, storage limits, and regional basis behavior.
  • Agricultural supply chains: planting cycles, processing throughput, export corridors, and inventory timing.
  • Secondary coverage: metals, logistics, infrastructure, and operating partnerships when they influence core commodity flow.

Operating model

Exposure is expressed through market positions, selective ownership stakes, and commodity-linked operating partnerships under one internal control framework.

Execution follows documented risk limits, counterparty review, invalidation thresholds, and ongoing portfolio supervision.
